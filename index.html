I am a Ph.D. student in the School of Electrical, Computer and Energy Engineering at Arizona State University and a member of the Performance and Resource Optimization in Networks (PROTON Lab), advised by Dr. Eirini Eleni Tsiropoulou. I received my B.Sc. in Computer Science with a Minor in Mathematics from Nazarbayev University in June 2024. Previously, I worked as a Research Assistant at the NU IoT Lab.

My research focuses on optimizing wireless network performance using game theory and reinforcement learning, with an emphasis on theoretical analysis and mathematical modeling.
